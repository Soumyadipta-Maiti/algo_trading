FinancialAdvisor.AI

A Subscription-based AI-driven Financial Advice and Analysis Platform

Project Summary

FinancialAdvisor.AI aims to develop a domain-specific language model for finance, offering personalized financial advice, data-driven trading pattern identification, strategy suggestions, and investment profile creation. Our subscription-based model will provide users with cost-effective access to expert advice and analysis.

Objectives

  1. Develop a domain-specific language model for finance that provides tailored financial advice.
  2. Analyze data to identify trading patterns and suggest effective trading strategies.
  3. Generate investment profiles for individual users based on their preferences and risk tolerance.
  4. Implement a user-friendly subscription model that offers fixed-cost access to financial advice and analysis.

Scope

  • Financial advice on stocks, bonds, mutual funds, ETFs, and other investment vehicles.
  • Analysis of historical price data and financial information for pattern identification.
  • Trading strategy suggestions based on technical and fundamental analysis.
  • Investment profile creation with portfolio allocation and risk management recommendations.
  • Mobile and web-based platform for easy access and real-time notifications.

Technical Requirements

  • Access to financial data sources, including historical stock prices and financial information.
  • Development of algorithms for identifying trading patterns and strategies.
  • GPT-based language model architecture, training, and fine-tuning for financial domain.
  • Secure, responsive, and user-friendly platform design and development.
  • Integration of security and privacy measures to protect user data.
  • Use of third-party APIs or services as needed.

Project Timeline

  1. Phase 1: Research and data collection (2 months)
  2. Phase 2: Algorithm and language model development (4 months)
  3. Phase 3: Platform design and integration (3 months)
  4. Phase 4: Testing and optimization (2 months)
  5. Phase 5: Launch and marketing (2 months)

Collaboration Details

We are seeking collaboration from individuals with the following expertise:

  • Financial analysts with deep knowledge of financial markets and trading strategies.
  • Data scientists experienced in natural language processing and machine learning.
  • Software developers proficient in platform design and integration.
  • Marketing specialists with experience in promoting financial services or tech platforms.

Budget and Resources

A detailed budget breakdown will be prepared, covering costs related to language model training, platform development, hosting, marketing, and other operational expenses.

Benefits and Unique Selling Points

  • Customized financial advice powered by a domain-specific language model.
  • Advanced data analysis for trading pattern identification and strategy formulation.
  • A cost-effective subscription model that allows users access to expert advice.
  • User-friendly platform with mobile and web-based access for convenient usage.
